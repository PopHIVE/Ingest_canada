Wastewater

The Canadian COVID-19 Wastewater Surveillance Program monitors viral signals in wastewater from treatment plants across Canada. The program tracks SARS-CoV-2 (N2 gene), Influenza A, Influenza B, and RSV. Data is collected from multiple monitoring sites and aggregated at site, provincial, and national levels on a weekly basis. The weighted average (w_avg) represents the population-weighted mean viral signal across sites within each geographic area.
